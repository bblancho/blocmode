== Description ==
Sukawati is fancy & elegant multi concept blog theme - it enables you to tailor body layout, headers, colors even fonts family easily with live editor (theme customizer). This theme has 4 built-in homepage Slider, 6 header layout, and also offer three content layouts: normal & grid (masonry).
